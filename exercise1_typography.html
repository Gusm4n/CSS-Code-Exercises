Look for Me

BY TED KOOSER

Look for me under the hood of that

old Chevrolet
settled in weeds at the end of the pasture.

I'm the radiator that spent its years bolted in front of an engine

shoving me forward
into the wind.

Whatever was in me in those days has mostly

leaked away
, but my cap's still screwed on tight

and I know the names of all these tattered moths and

broken grasshoppers
the rest of you've forgotten.
